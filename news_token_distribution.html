ERGO First Year Token

We have created the EFYT token (ERGO First Year Token) which will be converted to ERGO token (in 1:1 correspondence) soon after the genesis block is generated.

100K EFYT tokens have been distributed to Waves Community Token(WCT) holders. There are no immediate plans for the remaining tokens at present. There may be additional give-aways at some point in the future. All EFYT tokens not distributed by the time the ERGO genesis block is generated will be burned.
