ERGO First Year Token

Before the first ERGO block has been generated and for the first year afterwards the original development team will be creating the ERGO codebase and maintaining ERGO network. After that, the community will vote on developments and spendings with the help of privacy-friendly liquid-democracy system.

In order to fund development and maintenance miners will be taxed in a way similar to Zcash (with lower %%, 10% for the first year). During the first year the core team has the right to spend tokens needed for to bootstrap the network.

100M Ergo tokens will be issued within first year, thus the initial team will have 10M tokens. We have created the EFYT (ΣRGO first year token) token, which will be converted to ΣRGO token (in 1:1 correspondence) during the first year after the genesis block. 10M is issued, unused obligation will be burned.

First 100K EFYT tokens are to be spread among Waves Community Token(WCT) holders. Other tokens will be not touched in coming weeks. Will we see more give-aways? Maybe at some point! Tokens not distributed before Ergo genesis block will be burned. Feel free to contact us with any questions or ideas!
