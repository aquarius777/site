  • FAQ

  • Will there be an ICO?

    We haven’t decided yet if we will pursue an ICO or not. We expect to make that decision within the next few weeks and will make an announcement accordingly.

  • Will there be more airdrops to WCT holders?

    Any future airdrops will be done without any advance notice and would not be limited to WCT holders.

  • When will the ΣRGO network launch?

    There is no exact launch date at the moment. We will provide an estimated date as part of our yellow paper release. Right now we expect our testnet to be up and running in October 2017. Mainnet is targeted for May 2018.

  • Do you need translators?

    We will be seeking translators after white paper release.

  • Can I mine ΣRGO?

    Not yet as the mining algorithm isn't fixed yet. We will notify the community when mining can commence.

  • Will hard forks be possible in ΣRGO?

    Ergo will be focused on soft forkability and will also allow for updates to its programming language Sigma.

  • Will ΣRGO and Waves be cooperating or is ΣRGO a separate project?

    Though there will certainly be cooperation between the two projects on various levels, Σrgo is a completely separate and independent platform from Waves.
