ERGO Emission curve

After a lot of discussions with our community and inside the team, we are ready to finalize emission curve for Ergo blockchain after mainnet launch.

Emission curve was chosen based on these considerations:

  • - 100M tokens of final supply as was chosen at the beginning and written FAQ section
  • - 8 years of token distribution - due to Ergo economy model (see papers in TECH section) it is possible to issue all tokens quite fast
  • - 2 minutes block interval - to make network secure
  • - Fixed rate during first 2 years - this will make initial phase more predictable
  • - Reward reduction every 3 months, after first two years - this will make mining rewards more predictable

Taking all these ideas into consideration, we've chosen the following emission curve:

Concrete formula may be found in our github - it starts with 75 coins per block and this reward reduces for 3 coins every 3 months. Total supply in such a case is 97739925 coins while first year supply is 19710000 coins. Regarding this first years supply, we will burn 3029000 more EFYT tokens leaving 1971000 circulating EFYT tokens.
