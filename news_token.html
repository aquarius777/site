ΣRGO bootstrap strategy

ΣRGO will be an independent platform with its own blockchain. To make it truly independent it is supplied with a decentralized funding mechanism for development and maintenance. In order to achieve this we're going to implement treasury system in ΣRGO, similar to the one existing in ZCash or Dash - 90% of mining reward goes to block generator and the remaining 10% is distributed between community for ΣRGO software development, marketing, legal support and so on.

To launch ΣRGO as fast as possible its development will be controled by the bootstrapping team during the initial phase of ΣRGO life. By "initial phase" we mean the time before the first block in ΣRGO blockchain has been mined and the first year of its support, after that ΣRGO should be absolutely decentralized. Thus we believe that we have the right to dispose of first year of the treasury income and during first year of ΣRGO live, 10% of newly created coins will go directly to bootstraping team, becoming a truly decentralized treasury after that.

To raise funds for initial development, we've created a token (ΣFYF - ΣRGO first year futures), which will be converted to ΣRGO during the first year after the genesis block has been created. We chose Waves platform for ΣFYF issue because it have built-in decentralized exchange and allows to pay fees in token itself, so it's not required to control anything else but ΣFYF token. We are going to distribute it among our partners and sell it privately to raise funds for initial development and to prevent any legal issues. We are at the very beginning of ΣRGO platform, if you feel like you have something to contribute please do not hesitate to contact us.
