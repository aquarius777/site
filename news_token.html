ERGO Bootstrapping Strategy

The ERGO platform will be an independent blockchain. A significant cornerstone of this independence is a decentralized funding mechanism for development and maintenance. Principally, we will have a mining tax similar to ZCash with 90% of mining rewards going to miners and the remaining 10% to be allocated for ERGO software development, marketing, legal support, etc.

In order to launch ERGO as quickly as possible its development will be overseen by the bootstrapping team during the initial phase. By "initial phase" we mean the time between now and one year after the first ERGO blockchain block has been mined. We believe itâ€™s reasonable that the first year's mining tax be used by the bootstrapping team to fund all initial development activity. After that control over the ERGO platform should be absolutely decentralized.

We have created the EFYT (ERGO First Year Futures) token which will be converted to the ERGO token (in 1:1 correspondence) during the first year after the genesis block. The Waves platform has been chosen for EFYT issuance. The first batch has been distributed among Waves Community Token holders. Additional EFYT tokens may be given to partners or sold privately to raise funds for initial development. We intend to sell only as many as is absolutely necessary. We are in the very beginning stages of ERGO platform development. If you feel like you have something to contribute, please do not hesitate to contact us.
