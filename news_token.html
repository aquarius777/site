ΣRGO Bootstrapping Strategy

The ΣRGO platform will be an independent blockchain. A significant building block of the indepence would be a decentralized funding mechanism for development and maintenance. In the first place, we will have mining tax similar to one in ZCash, with 90% of mining rewards going to miners and the remaining 10% to be distributed for ΣRGO software development, marketing, legal support and so on.

To launch ΣRGO as fast as possible its development will be controled by the bootstrapping team during the initial phase of ΣRGO life. By initial phase we mean the time before the first block in ΣRGO blockchain has been mined, and also the one year after that. After that control over the ΣRGO platform should be absolutely decentralized. Thus we believe that we have the right to spend first year of the mining tax.

We have created the ΣFYF (ΣRGO first year futures) token, which will be converted to ΣRGO token (in 1:1 corespondence) during the first year after the genesis block. The Waves platform has been chosen for ΣFYF issuance. The first batch will be distributed among Waves Community Token holders, others could be given to partners or sell privately to raise funds for initial development (we will sell as minimum as possible though). We are at the very beginning of ΣRGO platform, if you feel like you have something to contribute please do not hesitate to contact us.
